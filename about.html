I am an Agile Software Developer, with a master degree in Computer Science. I love learning about different technologies and always be up to date.
During my working years I've noticed the lack of womens in the companies I've worked on. I wanted to do something about it, so I recently embarked on a mission to bring together all Italian Tech Girls, if you feel you are one of them then join here. Sometimes I do workshops on different things, where I help girls to learn programming!

I learn from the community and love to share what I have learned back. If you have any doubts about stuff in programming, or if you are a newbie and do not understand something or just want a body with whom to do some exercising, I have started recently an Ask Me Anything test. Just drop me a question and once a week I will get one and do a pair session with you.
Aaaand it's totally free :)

Just send me a message if you have a question about me or would like to get in touch.
